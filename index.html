Why this pricing is different

You should be able to prove value before you are asked for a commitment

Kabrios is built around self-service, employee empowerment, and AI-guided direction. That means the free tier has to do real work, not just show a dashboard. Your team should be able to complete a full workflow, see real traceability, and understand exactly how Kabrios helps them protect systems, protect clients, satisfy compliance, and move faster.

When you upgrade, you are paying for broader scale, broader visibility, and optional active engagement. You are not paying to unlock the product’s basic integrity.

What Kabrios does not do

  • No forced annual contract
  • No hidden add-on modules just to get basic value
  • No bait-and-switch free tier that cannot finish the job
  • No requirement to replace the tools you already use

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers, up front

Is the free tier a trial?

No. It is a real free tier built to let you complete one full lifecycle workflow and prove the value of the Kabrios operating model.

Are these plans month to month?

Yes. Kabrios is designed to be easy to start, easy to understand, and easy to continue because it keeps delivering value.

Do I need to replace my existing tools?

No. Kabrios is designed to fit around the tools you already use and turn them into a more usable operating rhythm.

What makes Managed different?

Managed adds active engagement. Your team still uses the same platform, but Kabrios helps keep the work moving with you instead of leaving you to run the program alone.

Can I upgrade later without losing my work?

Yes. The point of the free tier is to start in place and scale from there when your team needs more workflows, frameworks, reporting, or support.

Why put pricing in public?

Because buying security and compliance software should not require a mystery process. Kabrios should be easy to find, easy to trust, and easy to buy.
