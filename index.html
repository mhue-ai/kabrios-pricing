Pricing

Transparent pricing for real trust operations.

Kabrios is direct-to-consumer with public pricing, monthly billing by default, and annual discounts for teams that want a lower effective rate over a longer commitment.

The tiers are framework-driven: lighter trust programs should not buy the same package as regulated teams or customers that require full isolated delivery.

FAQ

Common pricing questions

Do you offer monthly billing?

Yes. Monthly billing is the default public model.

Do you also offer annual pricing?

Yes. Annual pricing is available as a discounted option for customers who want to commit up front.

Why four tiers?

Because a lighter SOC 2-oriented program should not buy the same package as a regulated customer or a buyer that needs isolated delivery. The tiers map to framework depth and delivery burden.

How are add-ons priced?

Support blocks, audit sprints, and staff augmentation have standard starting points. Custom integration work is quoted because complexity varies materially.

Which tier is the likely center of the business?

Core Compliance is likely to become the economic center over time because it covers the broadest practical SMB and growth-stage trust workload.
