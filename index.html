FAQ

Clear answers, up front

Does Kabrios offer a free tier?

Yes. It starts with Starter, a $0 tier designed to let you complete one full lifecycle workflow and prove the value of the Kabrios operating model.

How does annual pricing work?

A 1-year commitment costs 10 months of the monthly price. For example, $299/month becomes $2,990/year and $999/month becomes $9,990/year.

How many users are included by tier?

Starter includes 1 user, Core includes 5 users, and Program includes 25 users. Higher-trust arrangements are handled directly with Kabrios.

Do I need to replace my existing tools?

No. Kabrios is designed to fit around the tools you already use and turn them into a more usable operating rhythm.
