Does Kabrios offer a free tier?

Yes. It is a real free tier designed to let you complete one full lifecycle workflow and prove the value of the Kabrios operating model.

Are these plans month to month?

Yes. Kabrios is designed to be easy to start, easy to understand, and easy to continue because it keeps delivering value.

How should I interpret framework alignment by tier?

Higher tiers support broader frameworks, more workflows, more reporting, and more active operational support. They reflect program maturity, not just feature gating.

Do I need to replace my existing tools?

No. Kabrios is designed to fit around the tools you already use and turn them into a more usable operating rhythm.
